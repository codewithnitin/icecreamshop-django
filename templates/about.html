What is Nitin Ice Cream?

“Nitin Ice Cream” is the nickname used to describe the ice cream sold on the streets of Ranikhet,Manila. It is best known for being affordable, delicious, and produced using ice, salt, a steel pot, & a sturdy pair of hands. At Nitin Ice Cream, we seek to capture the simple goodness. We don’t want to simply serve ice cream; we want to create a cultural experience. Uttarakhand cuisine is synonymous with a tradition of creating and serving food with great love. Like Nanay (Mother) in her kitchen and the ice cream street vendor, We are here to carry on that tradition.

Our Story

Nitin Ice Cream is an ice cream shop, created as a job opportunity for our locals. Now, we are honored to train and employ many other amazing people with intellectual and physical exceptionalities. The ice cream shop is used to teach social skills, life skills, and employment skills for our amazing workers. Every employee goes through a certification program to become ICE CREAM EXPERTS! In the short time of Nitin Ice Cream's existence, we have found our ice cream shop is an innovative way to teach awareness, acceptance and the value of diversity; we’ve also found it adds a great sense of happiness and satisfaction to our customers. Ice cream makes people happy. Ice Cream served by our exceptional Ice Cream Experts brings smiles and joy to everyone who visits us! Nitin Ice Cream is the perfect way to reward those you serve, while investing in the lives of those we serve! Together, we can CHANGE THE WORLD ONE ICE CREAM AT A TIME!
